INeedCoffee is a project started by coffee fanatic Michael Allen Smith. The website was launched on April 4th, 1999, and is based in Seattle, Washington.

INeedCoffee aims to celebrate excellent coffee and empower coffee fans to make better coffee at home. This site hosts many tutorials related to coffee brewing and home coffee roasting.
